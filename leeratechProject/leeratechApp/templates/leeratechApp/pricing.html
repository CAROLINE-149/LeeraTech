Absolutely. Pay annually and save 20% on any plan (Basic, Standard, or Professional). Enterprise pricing is custom.

We offer flexible upgrades. You can move to the next tier anytime or request a custom quote for your exact student count.

No setup fees for Basic, Standard & Professional. Month-to-month billing with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime.

Yes! The Standard plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. Test all features with your real data.

Yes! We integrate M-PESA for fee collection and also accept bank transfers, cards, and mobile money for your subscription.
