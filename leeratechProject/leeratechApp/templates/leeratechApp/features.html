Features

LeeraTech simplifies school management with powerful tools for reports, attendance, fees, and timetables all in one easy-to-use platform.

LeeraTech automatically generates professional student report cards while analyzing academic performance.

  • Academic performance analysis
  • Student performance trends

Fee Management

Manage school finances easily with automated tracking and reporting.

  • Track student fee payments
  • Generate invoices
  • Monitor fee balances
  • Financial reports for administrators

Digitally record and monitor student attendance with automated reports.

  • Digital attendance recording
  • Daily and term attendance reports

  • Record competencies

Create organized school schedules automatically.
