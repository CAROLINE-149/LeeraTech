LeeraTech was born to free educators from administrative overload so they can focus on what truly matters: teaching and inspiring students.

Our story begins with a simple frustration

Founder Joshua Juma, while volunteering at local schools in Kenya, saw teachers buried under stacks of attendance sheets, fee ledgers, and report cards. Hours that should have been spent mentoring students were lost to manual paperwork. That’s when the idea struck: build an all-in-one platform that handles everything attendance, fees, timetables, and reports in one easy-to-use system. Today, LeeraTech is trusted by schools across the region, turning chaos into clarity.

Our Mission

To equip every school in Africa with powerful, intuitive tools that reduce administrative burden, improve transparency, and give educators back their most valuable resource time.

Our Vision

To become the most loved school management platform across Africa, serving 1,000+ schools by 2030 and transforming how education is administered at every level.
